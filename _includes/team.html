Brian specializes in developing personalized training programs specific to each individual client. As an accomplished junior, amateur and professional competitor, he takes a unique approach to player development. Rather than focusing on technical flaws, Brian emphasize the importance of critical moments in the swing. His core teaching philosophy is fueled by a passion to help morph golfers into the best players and people they can be, incorporating bio-mechanics, fitness, physiology, and performance psychology into a holistic lesson plan.
